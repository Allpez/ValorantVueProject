Valorant (stylized as VALORANT) is a tactical first-person shooter-style hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Development on the game began in 2014, and it was announced under the codename Project A in October 2019. A closed beta with limited access launched on April 7, 2020, and the game was officially released on June 2, 2020. Valorant was initially designed exclusively for Windows, but support for the Xbox Series X/S and PlayStation 5 consoles was added in June 2024, albeit without the possibility of cross-play between PC and console players.
